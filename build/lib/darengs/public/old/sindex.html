DARE-Rfold:
    DARE-Rfold represents a pipeline approach for RNA folding structure prediction. The overall design is shown below (Left Panel). The development of the entire pipeline is expected to take sometime and we plan to provide the service as incremental development is avialble. At this point, the RNA secondary structure predictions (Level I) using Sfold or RNAfold (in Venna RNA package) are available soon with our infrastructure. Our pipeline development is facilitated by incorporating the strategy to balance the scale-out with distributed computing and the scale-up with backend HPC grids. We build this infrastructure with Simple API for Grid Applications (SAGA) and our pilot-job abstraction known as SAGA-BigJob. The overall structure behind the pipeline is schematically illustrated below (Right Panel). At this point, we only provide the science gateway user interface for RNAFold (Vienna Pacakge)(March 2010).
